Accounting Education Mini-App

Ratio Analysis Dashboard

Enter simplified financial statement amounts to calculate common liquidity, profitability, solvency, and efficiency ratios. Use the results to practice interpreting financial performance and position.

How to use this app

Enter financial statement amounts below, then click Calculate Ratios. You may leave fields blank. The app will show “Need more data” when a ratio cannot be calculated.

You can also click Load Sample Company Data to quickly test the dashboard.

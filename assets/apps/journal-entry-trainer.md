# Journal Entry Trainer

Practice identifying the debit, credit, and explanation for common accounting transactions. Choose a topic, think through the entry, then reveal the answer.

This mini-app is designed for accounting students who want to strengthen their understanding of journal entries. It includes practice questions across several topics, including basic transactions, adjusting entries, accruals and deferrals, inventory, receivables, depreciation, bonds, leases, and revenue recognition.

[Open the Journal Entry Trainer]({{ site.baseurl }}/assets/apps/journal-entry-trainer/)

## How to use it

1. Choose a topic from the dropdown menu.
2. Read the transaction carefully.
3. Think through the accounts involved.
4. Decide which account should be debited and which account should be credited.
5. Click **Show Answer** to reveal the journal entry and explanation.
6. Mark yourself correct or incorrect to track your session score.

## Study prompts

As you work through each transaction, ask yourself:

- What account changed?
- Did the account increase or decrease?
- What is the normal balance?
- Should it be debited or credited?
